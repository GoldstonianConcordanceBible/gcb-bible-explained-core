# Seat-Time Methodology

This document explains how playlist content is translated into instructional time.

## Baseline Method

Instructional time is estimated using:

- total number of videos in a playlist
- average duration per video
- required reading time
- reflection/discussion time
- assignment completion time
- assessment time

## Formula

Total Course Hours =
Video Watch Time +
Assigned Reading Time +
Reflection Time +
Assessment Time +
Project/Essay Time

## Example

Playlist watch time: 120 minutes  
Reading time: 60 minutes  
Reflection time: 30 minutes  
Quiz time: 20 minutes  
Short essay: 90 minutes

Module total: 320 minutes = 5.33 hours

## Importance

This methodology is necessary because accreditation and audit review require a rational explanation of how instructional engagement is measured.

Playlists alone do not establish academic time. Structured academic tasks do.
